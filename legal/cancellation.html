MatConnect — Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

This policy applies to all bookings made through the MatConnect platform. By completing a booking, both Clients and Coaches agree to this policy.

Client Cancellations

Time Before SessionRefund Amount
48+ hours notice100% refund
24–48 hours notice50% refund
Less than 24 hoursNo refund
No-show (no notice)No refund

How to Cancel: Cancellations must be submitted through the MatConnect platform. Cancellations communicated directly to the Coach via text or phone do not constitute an official cancellation.

Coach Cancellations

If a Coach cancels a confirmed booking for any reason, the Client receives a 100% refund. Repeated Coach cancellations may result in account suspension.

No-Shows

Client no-show: No refund. The Coach is compensated for the scheduled session time.
Coach no-show: Client receives a 100% refund. Coach may face account review.

Subscription Refunds

Monthly: No refunds for the current billing period. Cancel anytime to stop future charges.
Annual: Prorated refunds within the first 7 days of the annual billing period. No refunds after 7 days.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the session date.

Disputes

If you believe a refund was incorrectly denied, submit a dispute through the Platform. MatConnect's decision on all refund disputes is final.
