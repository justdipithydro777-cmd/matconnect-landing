MatConnect — Community Code of Conduct

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

MatConnect is a community built on trust, safety, and respect. This Code of Conduct applies to all users — Coaches, parents, guardians, and athletes.

For All Users

Respect

  • Treat every person on the platform with dignity and respect, regardless of age, race, gender, religion, ability, or background
  • Communicate professionally and courteously through the platform's messaging system
  • Resolve disagreements calmly and through proper channels

Honesty

  • Provide truthful and accurate information in your profile and communications
  • Do not misrepresent your identity, credentials, or intentions
  • Do not fabricate reviews or ratings

Safety

  • Report any unsafe conduct, abuse, or violations immediately to MatConnect
  • Do not meet in unsafe or isolated locations without proper safety precautions

For Coaches

  • Maintain appropriate professional boundaries with all athletes at all times
  • Never engage in physical contact beyond what is necessary for safe wrestling instruction
  • Never conduct one-on-one sessions with a minor in a private or unobserved setting without parental authorization
  • Do not discuss or share inappropriate content with athletes or their families
  • Arrive on time; come prepared; do not arrive intoxicated
  • Coaches are strongly encouraged to complete SafeSport or equivalent safe sport training certification

For Parents and Guardians

  • Parents/guardians are encouraged to be present during training sessions, especially for younger athletes
  • Communicate your expectations and the athlete's limitations clearly to the Coach
  • Do not interfere with or undermine the Coach's instruction during sessions
  • Direct concerns to the Coach calmly and privately, or through the MatConnect platform

Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Use the "Report" feature on any profile or message
  • Email: matconnect777@gmail.com

Reports involving potential child abuse or criminal conduct will be referred to the appropriate authorities.

Consequences of Violations

Violations may result in a formal warning, temporary account suspension, permanent account ban, or reporting to law enforcement or child protective services.
