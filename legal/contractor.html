MatConnect — Coach Independent Contractor Agreement

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between MatConnect LLC ("Company") and the individual registering as a Coach on the MatConnect platform ("Coach").

1. Independent Contractor Relationship

Coach is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, or partner of MatConnect. As an independent contractor, Coach is not entitled to any employee benefits from MatConnect. Coach retains full control over how coaching services are performed, including methods, techniques, timing, and location.

2. Services

MatConnect grants Coach a non-exclusive license to use the Platform to list services, manage availability, communicate with Clients, and receive bookings. Coach agrees to provide coaching services in a professional, safe, and competent manner; maintain required licenses/certifications; respond to booking requests timely; and treat all Clients and athletes with respect.

3. Compensation

Coach sets their own rates. MatConnect may collect payment on Coach's behalf and remit applicable amounts after deducting platform fees. MatConnect will provide a 1099-NEC form to Coaches earning $600+ per calendar year. Coach is solely responsible for all taxes on income earned through the Platform.

4. Coach Representations

Coach represents that all profile information is accurate and truthful; they have the legal right to provide coaching services; they hold any licenses or certifications claimed; they will comply with all applicable laws; they have not been convicted of any crime involving violence, sexual misconduct, or child abuse; and they are legally authorized to work in the United States.

5. Prohibited Activities

Coach agrees NOT to: engage in abusive or harassing behavior; engage in sexual misconduct or inappropriate contact with any athlete or minor; solicit Clients to circumvent platform fees; misrepresent qualifications; accept direct payment for Platform-booked sessions; share Client information with third parties without consent; or discriminate against Clients on any protected basis.

6. Safety and Compliance

Coach is responsible for ensuring all training sessions are conducted in a safe environment. Coach acknowledges mandatory reporting obligations for child abuse under applicable state law. MatConnect may require background checks as a condition of continued platform access.

7. Termination

Coach may terminate by deactivating their account with 7 days' notice. MatConnect may terminate immediately for violations of this Agreement, receipt of credible misconduct reports, or any conduct that poses risk to users.

8. Indemnification

Coach agrees to indemnify and hold harmless MatConnect LLC from any claims, damages, and expenses arising from Coach's provision of coaching services, violations of this Agreement, any injury during a coaching session, or Coach's negligence or willful misconduct.

9. Governing Law

Disputes shall be governed by the laws of Minnesota and resolved through binding arbitration under AAA rules.
