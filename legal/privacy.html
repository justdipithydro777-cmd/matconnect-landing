MatConnect — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026 | Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

MatConnect LLC ("MatConnect," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the MatConnect platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account information (name, email, phone, photo), profile information (location, bio, certifications), payment information (handled by Stripe — we don't store card numbers), communications, and reviews.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage data, device information (IP address, browser type, OS), general location based on IP, and cookies/tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create and manage your account, facilitate bookings, process payments, send service-related communications, improve the Platform, detect fraud, comply with legal obligations, and display public profiles to other users.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Coach profiles are visible to Clients. We share information with trusted service providers (Stripe for payments, hosting, email, analytics) under strict confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting matconnect777@gmail.com. Some data may be retained for legal purposes.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

MatConnect is designed for adults (parents/guardians) on behalf of minor athletes. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe we've inadvertently collected such information, contact us at matconnect777@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies (required for function), analytics cookies (usage analysis), and preference cookies (settings). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Security

We implement SSL/TLS encryption, encrypted storage, and access controls. No method of transmission is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data. California residents have additional rights under CCPA. To exercise rights, contact matconnect777@gmail.com.
